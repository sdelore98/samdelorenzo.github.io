Smart Campus

Research Project conducted at Stevens Institute of Technology

I worked as a research assistant for Professor Gregg Vesonder of the Software Engineering Department. The project was to develop a device that would redefine spaces on campus by monitoring environmental data.

Our research paper was recently accepted into the IEEE Uemcon 2017 and published in the Xplore Digital Library.

Fighting For Happiness

A 2D, Single Player RPG Created in RPG Maker MV. Music created by Sam DeLorenzo, using LoopLabs.

It is the year 2075 and the company Evil Corp has created a new addictive device called the HAPPE that literally fabricates the very essence of happiness. Logan and his brother Oli have created a resistance called the Black Knights to destroy the HAPPE devices. Help the brothers shut down Evil Corp for good and save humanity!

Download the game here on itch.io

  • Date: May-August 2017
